# Bliss 🌿

> A wellness-first social media platform that combines health tracking, AI-powered nutrition analysis, stress monitoring, and emotionally adaptive content recommendations.

## 📖 Overview

Bliss reimagines social media by prioritizing user wellbeing over engagement metrics.

Traditional social media platforms are designed to maximize screen time and interactions. Bliss takes a different approach by using health and wellness data to personalize content that supports mental and physical wellbeing.

The platform combines:

* Health tracking
* AI-powered food analysis
* Stress monitoring
* Mood-based content recommendations
* Positive short-form video experiences

Instead of showing content that increases engagement, Bliss aims to show content that improves wellbeing.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

Current social media platforms often contribute to:

* Doom scrolling
* Anxiety
* Stress
* Comparison culture
* Digital addiction

Users consume content without considering their emotional or physical state.

Bliss introduces a new approach where content adapts to the user's wellbeing.

---

## 💡 Solution

Bliss integrates wellness metrics and AI-driven recommendations to provide a healthier social media experience.

The app analyzes:

* Daily activity levels
* Step count
* Sleep quality
* Nutrition intake
* Stress indicators
* User mood

Based on these factors, Bliss recommends content that helps users maintain a healthier digital lifestyle.

# ✨ Features

## 🏃 Home Dashboard

### Step Tracking

* Apple HealthKit integration
* Daily step count
* Activity progress tracking
* Wellness insights

### 🍽 AI Nutrition Scanner

Users can take a photo of their meal and receive:

* Estimated calories
* Protein content
* Carbohydrates
* Fat content
* Fibre content

### ❤️ Stress Monitoring

The application evaluates stress indicators using:

* Heart rate
* Sleep quality
* Activity levels

Stress status is displayed as:

* Low Stress
* Moderate Stress
* High Stress

### 🌟 Wellness Score

A unified wellness score generated from:

* Activity
* Nutrition
* Stress
* Sleep

---

## 🎥 Bliss Feed

A short-form video feed inspired by modern social media platforms but designed for wellbeing.

### Mood-Based Recommendations

Users select their emotional state:

* Happy
* Sad
* Stressed
* Tired

The feed adapts accordingly.

### Examples

#### Sad

* Positive stories
* Nature content
* Pet videos
* Encouraging messages

#### Stressed

* Breathing exercises
* Meditation videos
* Relaxation content

#### Happy

* Learning content
* Self-improvement
* Fitness motivation

### Digital Detox

Bliss encourages healthy usage by providing reminders when excessive scrolling is detected.
